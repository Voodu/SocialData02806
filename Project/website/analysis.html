Melbourne Job Hunt

Where to look for a job in capital of the Victoria state?

Slowly approaching the end of studies means starting looking for a job. Probably not many DTU students know about opportunities available in Australia, so we would like to present you the options in Melbourne, the capital city of the Victoria state. We hope that this will help you to choose the best industry and place to live in Melbourne if you decide to move to the other part of the world.

Firstly, does it make sense at all to work there? Are there new businesses opening?

It looks like Melbourne is constantly developing since 2002, so it is definitely worth some attention.

City areas

The city is big, nearly 10.000 km2, so it is easier to focus on some specific area. Taking into account only the central part, there are 13 small areas.

Availability analysis

Numbers of business establishments & jobs per area show what industry and which area may be the most promising.

Businesses per area

When it comes to the most promising areas in terms of business establishments, Melbourne (CBD) and Docklands are in the lead.

Jobs per area

Jobs number roughly correlates with the number of business establishments. Again, Melbourne (CBD) and Docklands have the highest growth between 2002 and 2019.

Businesses per industry

Most industries experienced some growth, but definitely Food and Beverage Services is an industry with the most stable and significant growth over the whole period 2002-2019.
